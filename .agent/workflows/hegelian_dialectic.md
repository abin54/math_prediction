# Hegelian Dialectic Logic Gate

This workflow transforms a linear prediction into a higher-order "Sublation" by resolving the conflict between data and chaos.

## Phase 1: The Thesis
Provide the most statistically likely, data-driven prediction based on the current 14-year (or 52-year) dataset. This represents the "Traditional Consensus" of the swarm.

## Phase 2: The Antithesis
Act as the "Chaos Factor." Argue that the Thesis is a result of **Overfitting** or **Pattern-Matching Noise**. Provide a counter-prediction that is mathematically valid but logically opposite to the Thesis.

## Phase 3: The Sublation (Synthesis)
Synthesize both layers. You must find a **Higher-Order Logic** that explains why both the Thesis and Antithesis appear true simultaneously. Identify the "Hidden Cycle" (e.g. planetary resonance or regime shift) that governs both.
