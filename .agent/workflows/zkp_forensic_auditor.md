# ZKP Forensic Auditor

This workflow transforms a prediction into a "Formal Proof" by identifying a 52-year invariant and proving why any other number breaks the rule.

Assume today’s Close number is NOT the predicted one. Prove why any other digit (0-9) would create a "Logical Singularity" or break the "Universal Rule" established in Phase 1.

## Phase 3: Adversarial Deposition
Explain the "Structural Pivots" of 1988 and 2004—years where the regime shifted. Prove that today's entropy state is mathematically linked to those pivots.

Admit "Incomplete Data" if the mathematical symmetry to the 1972 origin is less than 99%. Otherwise, provide the single-digit result and the absolute Proof.
