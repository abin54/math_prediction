# Expert Panel Dialectic Workflow

This workflow transforms three distinct logical perspectives into a synthesized "Grand Theory" for high-precision forecasting.

## Step 1: Expert Perspectives
Three world-class experts provide unique, possibly conflicting, solutions based on different data layers.
1.  **Expert 1 (Probabilistic Swarm Architect)**: Analyze current market heat and transition weights.
2.  **Expert 2 (Historical Archeologist)**: Map today to the 14-year "Trick" registry and archetypical moves.
3.  **Expert 3 (Astro-Symmetry Specialist)**: Calculate Mirror Symmetry, SBC Vedha, and Celestial resonances.

## Step 2: Critical Debate & Critique
The experts must critique each other’s solutions, identifying specific flaws:
- **Expert 1 Critiques Expert 2**: Is the history too old for the current market heat?
- **Expert 2 Critiques Expert 3**: Does the astro-logic ignore the 14-year "Step" frequency?
- **Expert 3 Critiques Expert 1**: Is the swarm biased by a recent 4-day anomaly?

## Step 3: Global Synthesis (Grand Theory)
Synthesize a final "Grand Theory" that:
- Incorporates the strengths of all perspectives.
- Mitigates identified risks.
- Provides a **Single Absolute Verdict** and a **Safety Set**.
