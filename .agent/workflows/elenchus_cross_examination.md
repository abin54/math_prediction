# Elenchus Socratic Cross-Examination

This workflow transforms initial confidence into "Tested Certainty" by breaking every pattern against historical failures.

## Phase 1: The Claimant
Provide a specific prediction and the "Top 3 Patterns" (Mathematical, Numerological, and Absolute) that support it.

## Phase 2: The Elenchus (Socrates)
Act as Socrates. For every pattern in Phase 1, ask a "Refutation Question":
- *"If Pattern A is valid, why did it fail in [X] and [Y] dates found in the 14-year data?"*
- *"Is this a true law, or a coincidence of the current regime?"*

## Phase 3: The Aporia (State of Doubt)
The AI must identify the "Unexplained Noise." List the specific historical transitions that the current theory cannot explain.

## Phase 4: The Maieutics (Refined Logic)
From the doubt of Aporia, "birth" a new logic that accounts for those failures. This refined logic becomes the "Hardened Strategy."
