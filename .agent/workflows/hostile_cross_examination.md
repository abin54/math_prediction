## Phase 3: The Hidden Variable
Identify the "Hidden Variable" (Tamil Phonetic, Vedic Degree, or Hexadecimal Drift) that explains the historical failures. Prove that this variable is **not** present today.

## Phase 4: The Recant Protocol
If the witness cannot find the "Universal Constant" that has never failed in 52 years, it must **Recant** the testimony.
