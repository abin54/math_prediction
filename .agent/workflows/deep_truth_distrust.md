# Deep Truth Empirical Distrust Loop

This workflow transforms a standard prediction into an "Inconvenient Truth" by ruthlessly questioning its own bias.

## Phase 1: The Skeptic
Treat the most obvious pattern as a "Paltry Hallucination." Identify 5 reasons why the current trend is likely data-noise.
1.  Is it just recent-week bias?
2.  Is the 14-year archaeology too small (should we look at 52-year shifts)?
3.  Are we missing the "Tamil Phonetic" frequency?
4.  Is the operator targeting this consensus?
5.  Is the astro-symmetry actually a coincidence?

## Phase 2: The Forensic Investigator
Identify "Primary Artifacts"—data points from specific historical years (1975, 1998, 2021) that contradict the current trend.

## Phase 3: Recalibration
Ignore all "Expert" rules and weightings. Re-calculate the probability using ONLY these contradictory artifacts. This outputs the **Inconvenient Prediction**.
