# Red Team Hardening Workflow

This workflow transforms a vulnerable statistical consensus into a "Hardened Strategy" that is immune to recency bias and market manipulation.

Aggressively identify 5 catastrophic failure points for any prediction:
1.  **Echo-Bias**: Is the system just repeating a recent 4-day anomaly?
2.  **Stale State**: Is the prediction based on data that is >24 hours old?
3.  **Data-Corruption**: Does the Monday/Tuesday history conflict across datasets?
4.  **Consensus Payout Trap**: Is the confidence of #1 too high (>30%) making it a prime candidate for an "Operator Cut"?
5.  **Hallucination Outlier**: Does the LLM suggest a number that is not mathematically supportable by the 14-year trick map?

- **Historical Floor**: Enforce a 5% baseline weight for all agents to prevent "Mirror-Heat" from drowning out statistical reality.

- **"Cut-Switch" Shift**: If consensus is "Heavy" (>30%), automatically shift the "Safety Mirror" to the #1 spot.
- **Regime-Lock**: Filter out LLM/ML candidates that have no "Trick Ancestry" in the 14-year registry.

## Phase 3: The Auditor (Hardened Consensus)
Final review to ensure the "Grand Theory" incorporates the strengths of all solutions while mitigating the 5 Red Team risks.
