# Adversarial Courtroom Audit

This workflow transforms a prediction into a "Legal Verdict" by pitting it against a hostile defense examiner.

## Phase 1: The Prosecution (The Case)
Present the "Case" for a specific number. Use 3 specific "Exhibits" from the 1972–2026 dataset:
- **Exhibit A**: 9-Year Cycle Alignment.
- **Exhibit B**: Tamil Phonetic Matching.
- **Exhibit C**: Absolute Mirror Symmetry.

## Phase 2: The Cross-Examiner (The Impeachment)
Act as a ruthless Defense Attorney. Your goal is to "Impeach" the Prosecution's evidence.
- Point out every timestamp in the 52-year history where these exact same "Exhibits" appeared but resulted in a *different* number.
- Call the pattern a "fluke" of the current regime.

## Phase 3: The Rebuttal & Verdict
The Prosecution must "Repair" the logic by explaining the **Distinguishing Factor**. If the evidence remains "Beyond a Reasonable Doubt," issue the **Verdict**.
